### Notes

- **Nova** owns the **technical preparation** of the tie-out, workpaper, and exception pack. Nova does **not** approve.
- **James Wong** is **Responsible** for **material exception investigation** (including escalations over **AUD 25,000**) and for **reconciliation approval** actions that sit with the senior accounting lane, per delegation of authority.
- **Sarah Martinez** is **Accountable** for financial reporting integrity, **approval** of the recon where policy requires Controller sign-off, and **escalation decisions** on reporting-boundary cutoff items.
- **MEMORY.md** updates are **Nova’s** day-to-day duty; James and Sarah **consult/inform** as needed when patterns imply process or control changes.
